Wigs

We made prototypes in many different kinds of fabrics, but the chiffon is the most ‘friendly’. When you first make them they’re very fluffy and then when you wash them a few times the silk chiffon starts to twist and ‘dread-up’ a bit and they go like dreadlocks, which is nice and I like them best when they’re about 3-4 weeks into the run because they’ve ‘collapsed’ a bit but they still move well.
